🔒 Privacy Policy

Fuel Complaint System — Pakistan

Last updated: March 2025

📋 Overview

The Fuel Complaint System is a WhatsApp-based service for citizens of Pakistan to report complaints about fuel pumps — including shortages, overpricing, pump closures, and illegal practices.

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it is protected. By using this service, you agree to the terms below.

📥 Information We Collect

When you submit a complaint through our WhatsApp bot, we collect:

  • CNIC number — for identity verification (stored in masked form)
  • WhatsApp phone number — automatically provided by WhatsApp; used to manage your conversation session
  • Location — GPS coordinates of the fuel pump you are reporting, along with derived province, city, and area
  • Fuel pump brand — selected from a list (e.g. PSO, Shell, Attock)
  • Nearest landmark — optional text you provide to help locate the pump
  • Complaint type — selected from predefined categories
  • Complaint details — free-text description of your complaint
  • Image — optional photo evidence you choose to upload
💡 We do not collect your full name, email address, or any information beyond what is listed above.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used exclusively to:

  • Register and track your fuel complaint
  • Generate a unique complaint ID for your reference
  • Route the complaint to the appropriate regulatory authority
  • Maintain a complaint record for follow-up and accountability

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

🗄️ Data Storage

Complaint data submitted via the WhatsApp bot is stored securely. Session data (your in-progress conversation) is held temporarily in server memory and is cleared when the session ends or the server restarts.

Your CNIC is displayed in masked form (e.g. 35202*****678) in all summary messages and is never displayed in full to anyone except authorised administrators.

🔗 Information Sharing

Complaint information may be shared with:

  • Relevant government or regulatory authorities responsible for fuel pump oversight in Pakistan
  • Internal system administrators for complaint management

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

💬 WhatsApp & Meta

This service uses the Meta WhatsApp Cloud API. When you message our bot, your messages are processed through Meta's infrastructure. Please review WhatsApp's Privacy Policy for details on how Meta handles your data.

We only access the message content you send to the bot and your WhatsApp phone number. We do not access your WhatsApp contacts or any other account information.

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data has been collected about you
  • Request deletion of your complaint data
  • Withdraw from the process at any time by simply not responding

To exercise these rights, please contact the system administrator.

📞 Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via the WhatsApp number listed on the home page.
