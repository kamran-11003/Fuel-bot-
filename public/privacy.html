1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Fuel Complaint System ("we", "our", "the System") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our WhatsApp-based complaint service. By using this service, you consent to the practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

When you register a fuel-related complaint via WhatsApp, we collect:

  • Phone number – Your WhatsApp number for communication
  • CNIC – National ID for verification (13 digits)
  • Region – Your selected region (e.g., Islamabad, Lahore)
  • Complaint details – Description of the fuel-related issue
  • Location – Pump location (coordinates or address)
  • Images – Optional photos you choose to upload

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and investigate your complaint
  • Contact you regarding your complaint status
  • Prevent duplicate or fraudulent complaints
  • Improve our services and analytics (in anonymized form)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (cloud database). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

Complaint records are retained as required by applicable government regulations. Session data used during the complaint flow may be cleared after submission. You may contact us to request deletion of your data where permitted by law.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Your complaint data may be shared with relevant government authorities for investigation and resolution. We may share data when required by law or to protect our rights.

7. WhatsApp

This service operates through Meta's WhatsApp Cloud API. Your use of WhatsApp is subject to Meta's Privacy Policy. We receive messages you send to our bot and respond accordingly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion where applicable
  • Withdraw consent for processing

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights, please contact the relevant government department overseeing the Fuel Complaint System.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
