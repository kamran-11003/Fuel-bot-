How it works

9 Simple Steps

Bot automatically guide karega — koi app download karne ki zaroorat nahi

1
👋
Greeting
Bot aapko welcome karega aur confirm karega ke aap shuru karna chahte hain ya nahi.
2
🪪
CNIC Enter Karein
Apna 13-digit CNIC number enter karein (bina dashes ke). Ye verification ke liye zaroor hai.
3
📍
Region Select Karein
List se apna city/region select karein — Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Lahore, Karachi, ya Other.
4
📝
Complaint Type
Complaint ki category select karein — fuel shortage, overpricing, pump closed, illegal practices, ya other.
5
📄
Details Likhein
Apni complaint ki tafseel apni zubaan mein likhein. Jitni zyada info utna behtar.
6
🗺️
Location Share Karein
Pump ki location WhatsApp location feature se share karein, ya address type kar dein.
7
📷
Photo (Optional)
Agar mumkin ho to pump/issue ki photo upload karein. Ya "Skip" press kar dein.
8
✅
Review & Submit
Sab details ek summary mein dikh jaenge. Sahi ho to Submit karein, galat ho to Edit karein.
9
🎉
Confirmation
Complaint register hone par unique ID milega jaise FC-00001 aur status "Pending" hoga.
