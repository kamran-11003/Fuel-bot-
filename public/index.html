📋 How It Works

1

🌐Choose Language

Select English or اردو — the bot will respond in your chosen language throughout.

2

🪪Enter Your CNIC

Provide your 13-digit CNIC number (no dashes). This verifies your identity.

3

📍Share Pump Location

Use WhatsApp's location pin to mark the fuel pump. We'll auto-detect your province, city, and area from the GPS coordinates.

4

🏪Select Pump Brand

Choose from PSO, Shell, Attock, Saudi Aramco, Total Parco, Hascol, or Other.

5

📌Nearest Landmark (optional)

Add a landmark near the pump to help authorities locate it quickly.

6

📝Complaint Type

Select: Fuel Shortage, Overpricing, Pump Closed, Illegal Practices, or Other.

7

📄Complaint Details

Describe what happened in your own words. More detail helps us act faster.

8

📷Upload Image (optional)

Send a photo as evidence. You can skip this step if you don't have one.

9

✅Review & Submit

Review a full summary of your complaint and submit. You'll receive a unique Complaint ID (e.g. FC-10234).
